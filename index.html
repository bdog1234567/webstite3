About Me

The Artist
Behind the Lens

VFX Artist & AI Content Creator

Hi, I'm Braden Di Mauro — the creator behind Nohea Media. The post-production industry is shifting fast, and AI is at the center of it. I believe learning these tools isn't about replacing the creator or the artist — it's about enhancing their vision with speed and precision.

That's why I actively integrate AI into my editing and VFX workflow, always looking for ways to streamline processes that used to take hours — from rotoscoping and compositing to motion tracking and content generation — so I can spend more time on the creative work that matters. Traditional craft meets modern tools, better results, faster delivery.
